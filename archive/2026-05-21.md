### 1. [Variance Reduction for Expectations with Diffusion Teachers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21489v1)

- 来源：arXiv
- 关键词：diffusion, distillation, expectations OVER noise levels, variance dominates compute COST, samples their estimator variance, techniques CUT gradient variance
- 链接：<https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21489v1>

**原始摘要**

Pretrained diffusion models serve as frozen teachers feeding downstream pipelines such as text-to-3D, single-step distillation, and data attribution.
